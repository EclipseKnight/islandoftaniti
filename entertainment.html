Entertainment

Take your breath away





Sightseeing

Most tourists flock to Taniti City, where native architecture and pristine white-sand beaches embrace the stunning Yellow Leaf Bay. Beyond lounging on the beach, visitors can embark on boat or bus tours around the island, hike through the lush rainforest, or witness the awe-inspiring spectacle of Taniti’s active volcano.






Water Activities

Discover the thrill of Taniti's fishing tours and snorkeling adventures! Enjoy chartered fishing tours to reel in the island's finest catches or dive into crystal-clear waters for snorkeling amidst vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life. These activities offer unforgettable ways to experience Taniti's natural beauty.






Casual

For those seeking a more casual experience, Taniti offers a delightful array of activities. Art enthusiasts can explore the island's art galleries, featuring works by local and international artists that capture the spirit and beauty of Taniti. Bowling provides a fun and relaxed way to spend an afternoon or evening with friends and family. History buffs will appreciate the local history museum, where fascinating exhibits and artifacts reveal the rich cultural heritage and storied past of the island. These options provide a perfect blend of culture, recreation, and relaxation for a leisurely day in Taniti.






Nightlife

Nightlife enthusiasts can enjoy lively pubs, sample local brews at a microbrewery, and dance the night away at a vibrant new club. Taniti's nightlife offers something for everyone.
