Island of Taniti





Welcome!

Enjoy our sun-kissed beaches, explore the lush rainforest, and visit the majestic volcano. Whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, or natural wonders, Taniti offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler. Start your journey and let the island's beauty captivate you.






Things to do

Dive into crystal-clear waters for snorkeling and scuba diving, hike lush forests to discover hidden waterfalls, or relax on sun-kissed beaches. Immerse yourself in local culture with traditional dances, culinary delights, and artisan markets. Whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, Taniti offers endless opportunities for unforgettable memories. Start your journey and let the island’s magic captivate you.






Restaurants

Enjoy fresh seafood, traditional dishes, and cozy cafes with stunning views. Taniti's diverse dining options promise a memorable culinary adventure.






Lodging

Choose from beachfront resorts, boutique hotels, cozy cottages, and eco-friendly bungalows. Enjoy stunning views, modern amenities, and warm hospitality. Find your perfect home away from home in Taniti.






Transportation

Easily explore the island with rental cars, scooters, bicycles, or public transport. Enjoy reliable and scenic options to make your travels around Taniti smooth and enjoyable.
